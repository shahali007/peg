About Us

PEG TECHNICAL SERVICE LIMITED is an indigenous company Incorporated in Nigeria.

With a solid team of experienced engineers, technicians and craftsmen of proven track records, our company has been able to demonstrate our proficiency in the execution of our projects in the most professional way taking cognizance of the safety of our personnel and materials.

Our strong management teams of professionals have always demonstrated our proficiency in the execution of our client’s project within specification.

PEG TECHNICAL SERVICE LIMITED is an engineering and inspection company registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission, with registration No. RC: 1227449.

PEG is one hundred percent indigenous and has carried out jobs on pipeline inspection, hydro test, Quality control and Quality assurance, Corrosion control Cathodic protection, Protective Coating , Drilling Inspection, Mechanical and Electrical Engineering, integrity management for companies involved in construction as well as oil servicing firms.

PEG is licensed by the Department of Petroleum Resources (DPR) to carry out pipeline inspection, hydro test, Quality control and Quality assurance, Corrosion control Cathodic protection, Drilling Inspection, Mechanical and Electrical Engineering, integrity management on plant items-flow-lines and fabricated structures.

PEG is determined to render services and supply materials that will not only conform to the acceptable standard but will also satisfy the need of her client in the field of activities. Theoretical and practical knowledge of the non-destructive test they perform, supervise and evaluate. Our personnel are trained in accordance with ISO 9712 (International Standard for certification for non-destructive testing personnel).
